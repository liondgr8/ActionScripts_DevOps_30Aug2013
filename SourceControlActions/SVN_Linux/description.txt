SVN Checkout:

sh svnCheckOut.sh <pathWhereToDownload> <svnRepoPath> <usernameRepo> <password>




SVN Commit:

sh svnCommit.sh <pathOfProject> <message>



SVN Export:

sh svnExport.sh <pathWhereToDownload> <svnRepoPath> <usernameRepo> <password>




SVN Import:

sh svnImport.sh <sourceProjectPath> <repoProjectPath> <message>



SVN Update:

sh svnUpdate.sh <pathOfProject> <revision>
